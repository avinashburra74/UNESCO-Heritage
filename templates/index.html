UNESCO HERITAGE PROJECT

The UNESCO Heritage Project is dedicated to identifying and preserving cultural and natural heritage around the world. Since 1972, we have worked to promote international collaboration for the conservation of World Heritage sites. Our efforts ensure that ancient monuments and natural parks are protected for future generations. Join us in safeguarding these treasures, which represent the diversity and history of our shared human experience and the natural beauty of our planet.
